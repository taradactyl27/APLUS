CDPAP
CDPAP (Consumer Directed Personal Assistance Program) is an innovative program available to Medicaid eligible consumers in need of home care. They direct their own care and choose their own caregiver. The paid caregiver can be a friend, neighbor, aide or family member under this win-win program. These familiar and trusted individuals DO NOT need a home health aide certificate or any other qualification. They are called Personal Assistants.
